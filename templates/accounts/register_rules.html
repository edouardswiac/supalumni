Another network ?

At this time there is no true SUPINFO alumni network, where you can easily keep an eye on your fellow students' professional activities. This network aims to be the top alumni network within SUPINFO. To achieve this ambitious goal, it relies on the participation of the students to fill the directories and spread the word.

How may I contribute ?

Anything you can do on this website is listed in your account. You can edit your personnal informations to display your LinkedIn or Twitter URL, or to expose your professional objectives. As a new user, click on Add a company to register your current or former companies in the directory. Once created, add the position you held. That's it! You can manage your positions in Manage positions.

You can also bookmark profiles. When visiting a profile, click Bookmark profile. Bookmarks are not a mutual relationship and are private. If you bookmark me, I won't know
